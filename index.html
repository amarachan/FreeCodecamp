In Memory of Stephen Hawking

The man who studied the universe

Theoretical physicist, Cosmologist



Timeline of Important Events in Sir Stephen William Hawking's Life:


  • 1942 January 8th Hawking was born in Oxford.
  • 1952 September the Hawking has attended the St Albans Grammar School.
  • 1959 October at the age of 17 Hawking began his university education at University College, Oxford.
  • 1962 October Hawking recieved degree in natural science from University College, Oxford.
  • 1963 November for the first time he was diagnosed with motor neurone disease.
  • 1965 July Hawking is married to Jane Wilde.
  • 1966 January he received a research fellowship at Gonville and Caius College.
  • 1966 March Stephen Hawking has obtained his PhD degree from Trinity Hall at Cambridge in applied mathematics,theoretical physics and general relativity, cosmology also won the prestigious Adams Prize.
  • 1969 he has accepted specially created fellowship for Distinction in Science to ramain in Gonville and Caius College.
  • 1970 he postulated what became known as the second law of black hole dynamics, that the event horizon of a black hole can never get smaller.
  • 1973 Hawking's first book, The Large Scale Structure of Space-Time was published.
  • 1974 Hawking was elected for Fellow of the Royal Society.
  • 1975 he was awarded both the Eddington Medal and the Pius XI Gold Medal.
  • 1976 recieved Dannie Heineman Prize, the Maxwell Prize and the Hughes Medal.
  • 1979, Hawking was elected for Lucasian Professor of Mathematics at the University of Cambridge.
  • 1981 he was awarded the American Franklin Medal.
  • 1985 he received further academic recognition, including five more honorary degrees and the Gold Medal of the Royal Astronomical Society.
  • 1987 he recieved the Paul Dirac Medal.
  • 1988 March Stephen Hawking's popular book A Brief History of Time is published.
  • 1988 Stephen Hawking jointly with Roger Penrose, the prestigious Wolf Prize.
  • 1995 Hawking gave divorce to jane Wilde and married to Elaine Mason.
  • 2006 Hawking was awarded Copley Medal from the Royal Society.
  • 2006 Hawking and Elaine Mason quietly divorced.
  • 2007 Hawking and his daughter Lucy published the book named George's Secret Key to the Universe.
  • 2008 received the Fonseca Prize.
  • 2013 recives Fundamental Physics prize .
  • 2014 A film named The Theory of Everything was made based up on the Sir Stephen Hawking's life.
  • 2018 March 18th Sir Stephen William Hawking died in Cambridge, England.
